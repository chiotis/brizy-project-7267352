INFORMATIONAL BULLETIN
Payment & Visa Info for delegations

VISA

  • For any assistance please contact the organizing committee at info@wuccheergreece2022.com

Fees

  • A Participation Fee of €70 per person per night is due to the Organization Committee.

This fee includes the participation fee, accommodation, meals and transportation (from the airport to the hote and vice versa / from the hotel to the competition area and vice versa).


Payment deadlines

  • Three months before the event (3/8/2022 the latest) universities entering the competition must submit a deposit of 25% of the total cost.
  • The rest of the payment will be invoiced and is due 14 days before the start date of the event (19/10/2022 the latest). There will be the option of payment upon arrival following a consultation with organizing committee.


For any further information please contact the OC at info@wuccheergreece2022.com
