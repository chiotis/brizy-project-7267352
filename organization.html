Eleftherios Avgenakis 

Deputy Minister of Culutre and Sports of Greece

I greet the 2022 FISU University World Cup Cheerleading, which will be held in Heraklion, Crete. A great sports event, with the participation of thousands of student-athletes from dozens of Universities globally.


The implementation of this event is a great opportunity for the promotion of the sport of cheerleading, which in recent years, has shown particular growth in Greece, taking into account that more and more young children choose to engage in it regularly. Cheerleading is an extremely spectacular sport, that combines fitness with entertainment.


The Deputy Ministry of Sports of Greece invests in hosting sports events of international scope, such as the 2022 FISU University World Cup Cheerleading. In this way we strengthen the prestige of the sports horizontally and enhance their reputation to the fans. International events are the lever for the development of sports tourism, which brings multiple benefits to strengthen the economy and productive development of our country.


The city of Heraklion is ready to welcome this great event and to offer all the visitors, athletes, coaches and members of the Universities an unforgettable hospitality. The region of Crete in general, with its huge cultural heritage, hospitality, gastronomy and natural wealth will assure that all the visitors will leave our country with the best memories.


I am sure that the event will be competitive, thanks to the cooperation and the excellent effort of the International University Sports Federation, the Hellenic Sports Federation of Cheerleading, the Organizing Committee and the participants.


I wholeheartedly wish good luck to the athletes.
